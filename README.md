# Sales Analytics Dashboard (Excel)

## Project Overview

This project presents an interactive Sales Analytics Dashboard built in Microsoft Excel to analyze sales performance across regions, customer segments, products, and shipping modes. The dashboard provides key performance indicators (KPIs), revenue trends, customer insights, and operational metrics to support data-driven decision-making.

## Tools & Skills Used

- Microsoft Excel
- Power Query
- Pivot Tables
- Pivot Charts
- Slicers
- Data Cleaning & Transformation
- KPI Design
- Dashboard Development
- Business Analysis

## Data Preparation

Before building the dashboard, Power Query was used to clean and transform the dataset. Several date fields contained inconsistent formats, which were standardized to ensure accurate time-based analysis. Additional fields such as Year and Quarter were created to support trend analysis and reporting.

## Dashboard Features

- Total Sales by Year
- Sales Growth Analysis
- Overall Sales Growth
- Total Orders
- Revenue by Segment
- Revenue by Region
- Top 10 Customers by Revenue
- Top 5 Products by Revenue
- Shipping Mode Distribution
- Category Revenue by Year
- Quarterly Sales Trend Analysis
- Interactive Slicers for Category, Region, Segment, and Ship Mode

## Key Insights

- Total Sales reached approximately **$2.3M** during the analysis period.
- Overall Sales Growth reached **50.5%** from 2016 to 2018.
- The **West Region** generated the highest revenue among all regions.
- The **Consumer Segment** contributed **50.76%** of total revenue, making it the largest customer segment.
- **Standard Class** was the most frequently used shipping mode, accounting for **59.91%** of all shipments.
- Sales demonstrate a clear seasonal trend, with **Q1 consistently recording the lowest sales levels** and **Q4 achieving the highest sales levels**, indicating stronger sales performance toward the end of each year.
- Revenue increased steadily across all product categories, with **Technology** generating the highest revenue by 2018.
